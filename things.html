Things To Do in New Braunfels

New Braunfels offers a wide variety of activities year-round. Historic Gruene is a must-see, featuring Texas’ oldest dance hall, charming antique stores, and riverside dining. Live music fills the air almost every night, and the rustic atmosphere attracts visitors from around the world seeking authentic Texas culture.

Schlitterbahn is another top attraction, offering miles of water slides, tube chutes, and lazy rivers. It’s perfect for families looking for a full day of summer fun.

Seasonal events like Wurstfest, Wassailfest, and the Downtown Christmas Market draw thousands each year. Whether you enjoy food, live music, shopping, or simply soaking in the historic scenery, New Braunfels delivers a memorable experience.
